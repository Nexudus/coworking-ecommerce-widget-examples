Our Locations

Three unique spaces, one standard of excellence. Find your Kalkio — and make it yours.

Kalkio Central

1 Central Street, City Centre, EC1A 1AA

Our flagship location, right in the heart of the city. Flooded with natural light through floor-to-ceiling windows, Kalkio Central is home to a vibrant mix of startups, freelancers, and established businesses. The rooftop terrace is a favourite for lunch breaks and informal meetings.

Hot Desks Dedicated Desks Private Offices 4 Meeting Rooms Rooftop Terrace 24/7 Access Café On-Site

Reception hours: Mon–Sat 07:30–22:00  |  24/7 access for members

Kalkio Riverside

12 Waterfront Lane, Riverside District, SE1 7PQ

With sweeping river views from every desk, Kalkio Riverside is our most sought-after location. This converted warehouse space blends original brick, exposed beams, and lush indoor plants with the latest workplace technology. The event hall downstairs hosts over 80 events a year.

Hot Desks Dedicated Desks Private Offices Event Hall (80 cap.) River Views Bar & Café Bike Storage Showers

Reception hours: Mon–Sat 07:30–22:00  |  24/7 access for members

Kalkio East

7 Maker's Yard, East Creative Quarter, E2 9HJ

In the heart of the city's creative quarter, Kalkio East attracts designers, developers, filmmakers, and makers. The studio-style open plan is flexible and expressive, with a professional photography studio, podcast recording booth, and a dog-friendly policy that keeps the atmosphere relaxed.
