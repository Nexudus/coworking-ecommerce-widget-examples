FAQ

Common questions

Is the tour really free?

Yes, completely. There's no commitment, no sales pressure — just a genuine chance to see the spaces, meet the team, and ask any questions.

How long does a tour take?

Most tours last between 30 and 45 minutes, depending on how many questions you have. If you'd like to go deeper on a particular space or pricing, we're happy to spend longer.

Can I trial a desk before committing?

Yes — we offer a day pass option so you can experience the space before signing up for a membership. Ask your host about this during your tour.

Do you have parking?

All Kalkio locations are well-connected by public transport. Paid parking is available nearby at each site, and Kalkio East has limited on-street parking. We also offer secure bike storage at all locations.

Are the spaces accessible?

Yes. All Kalkio locations are step-free accessible, with lift access to all floors, accessible toilets, and adjustable desks available. Let us know of any specific requirements when you book and we'll make sure everything is ready.

Prefer to speak to someone first?

Our team is happy to answer questions before you visit. Get in touch and we'll come back to you quickly.
